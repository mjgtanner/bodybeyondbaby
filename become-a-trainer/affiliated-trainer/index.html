About

Our Affiliates are the go-to mum-focused fitness businesses in their geographical area.

Through our high standards and quality control together we are on a mission to lead the way in ensuring as many women as possible are looked after safely and effectively.

Benifits

Join a like-minded team of fitness professionals and businesses that share the same mission to ensure all women are looked after safely and effectively after having a baby.

  • Work alongside Women’s Health Physiotherapists to ensure your clients gain the best possible care.
  • Become the Body Beyond Baby go-to fitness professional or business working with mums in your area.
  • Monthly upskilling sessions in both the business and technical development space.
  • Benefit from key marketing material and campaign timelines.

Pre-requisites

  • Certificate 3 & 4
  • Safe Return to Exercise Accredited
  • Appropriately insured
  • Alignment with your fitness industry governing body
  • Proving your ongoing relationship with a Women’s Health Physio – (we can help implement this)

Once the Pre-requisites are met please fill out our application form to ensure you are a great fit (and we are for you too!) and that you are not in conflict with any of our current affiliates.
